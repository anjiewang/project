About


The Youtube Influencer Search tool allows you to search and filter for influencers in your niche
via keywords or related videos, returning a list of leads that you can save, enrich, and export.



Features



Search

Search for influencers by keyword or related videos and get back relevant channels with channel statistics

Save

Save influencer channels to lists and organize leads in your personal dashboard

Enrich

Enrich Youtube channel stats with Instagram username and follower statistics

Export

Export all results and saved lists to a CSV
